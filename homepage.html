User guide
How to use ScamShield
1
Choose "Scan ad URL" for links or "Paste ad copy / SMS" for text messages.
2
Paste the suspicious link, domain, or message text into the input bar.
3
Hit "Audit ad link" — results appear within a few seconds.
4
Read the safety score and flags. Red = danger. Green = safe. Yellow = caution.
5
Tap "Broadcast alert" to share the result on WhatsApp with your contacts.
Tips for best results
Always paste the full URL including https:// — shortened links (bit.ly etc.) can also be scanned.
For SMS scams, paste the entire message text including any urgency phrases for the most accurate analysis.
A score below 30 is a strong scam signal. Scores above 80 indicate a clean verified source.
Never click a link you're unsure about — paste it here first and let the AI check it safely.
Your scan history is saved locally in this session so you can review past results from the dashboard.
